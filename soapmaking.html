The first concrete evidence we have of soap-like substance is dated around 2800 BC., the first soap makers were Babylonians, Mesopotamians, Egyptians, as well as the ancient Greeks and Romans. All of them made soap by mixing fat, oils and salts. In this elective they have made two different types of soap andthis work was handled by Mrs.Onyolu our vice principal.
