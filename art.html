TIE & DYE

Welcome to our world of colors where colors used to create one of the best fashion outfits. Right now our recent outfits are based on tie and dye.It is a very unique style of design.

TIPS NEEDED TO CREATE A GOOD BLEND OF COLORS IN TIE AND DYE

  • Know your differrent shades of colors.
  • Be careful with your colors.
  • Be creative and imaginative.
  • Always get the right materials and organize them before using them.

DESIGNS

Tie and dye has different designs which include;

  • spiral design
  • Crumple.
  • Bullseye.
  • Sunburst.
  • Horizontal Stripes.
  • Diagonal Stripes.
  • Vertical Stripes.
  • Box Folds.
MATERIALS NEEDED IN TIE AND DYE
  • 100% Cotton T-shirt (anything containing 5% or more of polyester will not dye well)
  • Soda Ash.
  • Rubber Bands.
  • Zip Ties.
  • Rack (to dye on)
  • Plastic Bin (to catch the dye)
  • Fiber Reactive Dye.
  • Ziploc Bag.
